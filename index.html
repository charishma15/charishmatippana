Launch - Worksite Monitoring System

In the pharma industry, mistakes have very serious consequences, so progress will always have to be slow and cautious. From chemical exposure and chemical equipment accidents, heatstroke, there are numerous dangers facing the pharma industry. Robust protocols and massive training alongside personal safety equipment (PPE) are important. However, these practices do not inform employees or workers working near reactor areas when external environments quickly turn hazardous. Similarly, managers or supervisors are completely clueless when remote reactor area workers or employees are reaching their physical thresholds. Ensuring industrial safety is an endless task. This project offers an IoT solution that minimizes the risk of health hazards and industrial accidents, reduces costs, and increases or develops productivity. This introduces an industrial safety support solution that improves industrial safety and works efficiency, both of which are required in various industrial fields.
